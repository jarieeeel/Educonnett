1ro de Bachillerato – Ciencias

Learning a Global Language

La asignatura de English en primer año de Bachillerato fortalece las habilidades comunicativas básicas en el idioma inglés. Los estudiantes desarrollan la comprensión oral y escrita, así como la capacidad de expresarse en situaciones cotidianas, preparando una base sólida para el aprendizaje académico y profesional.

Main focus: Basic communication, vocabulary, grammar and understanding simple texts in English.

Contents – 1st Year

  • Greetings and personal information.
  • Basic grammar structures.
  • Vocabulary related to daily life.
  • Simple reading and listening activities.
  • Writing short texts and dialogues.
  • Pronunciation and basic conversation.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand basic spoken and written English.
  • Communicate simple ideas clearly.
  • Expand everyday vocabulary.
  • Develop confidence when speaking English.
  • Prepare students for higher academic levels.

Advantages and Challenges

Advantages

  • Improves communication skills.
  • Essential for future studies and careers.
  • Facilitates access to global information.
  • Encourages cultural awareness.

Challenges

  • Pronunciation may be difficult at first.
  • Requires constant practice.
  • New vocabulary needs memorization.
